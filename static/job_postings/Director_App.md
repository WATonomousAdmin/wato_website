> **Note:** This is an executive leadership position at WATonomous. Project Directors lead either an administrative vertical or a technical project, reporting directly to the Co-Captains. Previous leadership experience is beneficial but not required per our constitution.

> **Application Note:** You can apply to this role if you demonstrate leadership ability and passion for the project area. You are also welcome to directly contact the current leadership team to express your interest and learn more about available director positions.

## Description

WATonomous is seeking passionate leaders to serve as Project Directors or Co-Directors for our various technical projects and administrative divisions. As a Director, you will lead one of our major initiativesâ€”whether that's an autonomous vehicle project, a business operations division, or another key organizational vertical. You'll define goals, manage timelines, oversee team members, and be accountable to the Co-Captains for both project progress and team member well-being.

This role offers an exceptional opportunity to gain hands-on leadership experience while driving innovation in autonomous robotics. Co-Director positions are available to enable collaborative leadership models where two directors share equal authority.

## Responsibilities

As a Project Director at WATonomous, you will:

- **Strategic Planning:** Define goals, timelines, and success metrics for your domain
- **Team Leadership:** Recruit, manage, and mentor team members within your project
- **Process Development:** Establish and refine workflows and processes for your team
- **Resource Management:** Allocate resources effectively and advocate for project needs
- **Stakeholder Communication:** Report progress to Co-Captains and present to sponsors
- **Cross-Project Collaboration:** Work with other directors to share resources and knowledge
- **Member Well-being:** Ensure team members are engaged, learning, and not overwhelmed
- **Technical/Administrative Excellence:** Drive innovation and quality in your project area
- **Succession Planning:** Develop future leaders within your team
- **Budget Management:** Oversee project budgets and funding allocation

This is a full-time leadership position requiring approximately 10-15 hours per week, including:
- Weekly team meetings with your project members
- Bi-weekly director meetings with leadership team

For Co-Director positions:
