> **Note:** This is a leadership role focused on ensuring project success and aligning technical achievements with marketing opportunities. We are looking for organized individuals who can bridge the gap between engineering execution and public visibility.

## Description

WATonomous is seeking a dedicated Project Manager to join our Leadership and Management team. In this role, you will oversee the successful delivery of our robotics projects while ensuring they create maximum impact for our marketing and outreach efforts. You'll work at the intersection of technical project management and strategic communication, helping translate engineering milestones into compelling stories that attract sponsors, recruits, and recognition.

This position offers a unique opportunity to develop project management skills in a fast-paced, technical environment while learning how to leverage project outcomes for organizational growth and visibility.

## Responsibilities

As a Project Manager at WATonomous, you will:

- **Project Planning & Execution:** Create and maintain project roadmaps, timelines, and deliverables for robotics initiatives
- **Marketing Alignment:** Identify project milestones and achievements that can be leveraged for marketing content
- **Cross-functional Coordination:** Facilitate communication between technical teams and marketing/business operations
- **Progress Tracking:** Monitor project status and provide regular updates to leadership and stakeholders
- **Risk Management:** Identify potential roadblocks and develop mitigation strategies
- **Resource Allocation:** Work with division leads to ensure optimal resource distribution across projects
- **Documentation:** Maintain project documentation that can be easily translated into marketing materials
- **Event Planning:** Coordinate project showcases, demos, and presentations for sponsors and public audiences
- **Success Metrics:** Define and track KPIs that demonstrate both technical and marketing value
- **Content Creation Support:** Work with marketing team to develop project-based content for social media, website, and sponsors

## Key Qualifications

### Required:
- Strong organizational and time management skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication abilities
- Ability to understand technical concepts and translate them for non-technical audiences
- Experience with project management tools (Notion, Jira, Asana, or similar)
- Collaborative mindset with ability to work across diverse teams
- Problem-solving skills and adaptability

### Nice to Have:
- Previous project management experience in technical or engineering contexts
- Familiarity with Agile/Scrum methodologies
- Experience with content creation or marketing
- Understanding of robotics or autonomous systems
- Knowledge of social media platforms and digital marketing
- Photography/videography skills for documenting projects
- Experience presenting to stakeholders or public audiences
- PMP, CAPM, or other project management certification
- Background in engineering, computer science, or related fields
