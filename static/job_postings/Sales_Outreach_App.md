> **Note:** This is a critical business development role within WATonomous. We are looking for driven individuals who can secure funding and build partnerships to support our robotics innovations.

## Description

WATonomous is seeking an enthusiastic Sales & Outreach Coordinator to join our Business Operations team. In this role, you will be responsible for securing both internal university funding and external corporate sponsorships that enable our team to build cutting-edge autonomous robots. Your efforts will directly impact our ability to compete internationally and push the boundaries of student-led robotics innovation.

This position offers a unique opportunity to develop professional sales and relationship management skills while working with a technical team at the forefront of autonomous systems. You'll interact with industry leaders, university administrators, and funding organizations to secure the resources that make our ambitious projects possible.

## Responsibilities

As a Sales & Outreach Coordinator at WATonomous, you will:

- **Sponsorship Acquisition:** Identify, contact, and secure corporate sponsors for our various robotics projects
- **Internal Funding:** Navigate university funding opportunities including WEEF, MEF, and departmental grants
- **Relationship Management:** Build and maintain long-term relationships with sponsors and funding partners
- **Proposal Development:** Create compelling sponsorship packages and grant proposals that showcase our value proposition
- **Partnership Strategy:** Develop strategic partnerships that provide both funding and technical resources
- **Communication:** Serve as the primary liaison between sponsors and our technical teams
- **Reporting:** Track sponsorship metrics and provide regular updates on funding progress
- **Market Research:** Identify new funding opportunities and stay informed about industry sponsorship trends
- **Collaboration:** Work with marketing team to create sponsor-focused content and recognition materials

- Real-world B2B sales and business development experience

## Funding Targets & Opportunities

You'll work on securing funding from various sources:
- **Corporate Sponsors:** Tech companies, automotive manufacturers, engineering firms
- **Internal Funding:** WEEF (Waterloo Engineering Endowment Foundation), MEF (Mathematics Endowment Fund), department grants
- **Government Grants:** NSERC, Ontario Centers of Excellence, innovation grants
- **In-Kind Donations:** Software licenses, hardware components, cloud credits
- **Competition Prizes:** Identifying and applying for relevant competitions with monetary awards
