> **Note:** This is a mid-level leadership position at WATonomous. Squad Leads manage specific technical or administrative squads within our larger divisions, serving as the bridge between team members and directors.

> **Application Note:** Current WATonomous members who have shown initiative and technical/administrative expertise are encouraged to apply. You can also directly contact your division director to express interest in squad lead opportunities.

## Description

WATonomous is seeking dedicated Squad Leads to manage focused teams within our technical projects and administrative divisions. As a Squad Lead, you'll be responsible for a specific area of workâ€”such as perception algorithms, path planning, hardware integration, social media, or sponsor relations. You'll guide a small team of 3-8 members, ensuring successful delivery of your squad's objectives while fostering member growth and development.

This role offers an excellent stepping stone into leadership, providing hands-on management experience while maintaining involvement in technical or administrative work. Squad Leads report to Project Directors and work closely with other squad leads to ensure seamless integration across the organization.

## Responsibilities

As a Squad Lead at WATonomous, you will:

- **Technical/Administrative Leadership:** Guide your squad's work and ensure quality deliverables
- **Task Management:** Break down project goals into actionable tasks for squad members
- **Mentorship:** Provide technical guidance and career development support to squad members
- **Progress Tracking:** Monitor squad progress and report to your Project Director
- **Cross-Squad Coordination:** Collaborate with other squad leads to integrate work
- **Knowledge Transfer:** Document processes and ensure knowledge sharing within your squad
- **Recruitment Support:** Help identify and onboard new members for your squad
- **Meeting Facilitation:** Run effective squad meetings and working sessions
- **Quality Assurance:** Review squad outputs and maintain high standards
- **Innovation:** Propose new approaches and improvements within your domain

## Key Qualifications

### Required:
- Active WATonomous member with demonstrated commitment

- Good communication and interpersonal abilities
- Ability to break down complex problems into manageable tasks

- 1+ terms of experience in WATonomous

- Familiarity with agile/scrum methodologies

## Time Commitment

This is a part-time leadership position requiring approximately 8-12 hours per week, including:
- Weekly squad meetings (1-2 hours)
- Regular one-on-ones with squad members
- Technical/administrative work within your domain
- Coordination meetings with other squad leads
- Monthly meetings with your Project Director
- Documentation and planning time

## Squad Success Metrics

Your performance will be evaluated based on:
- Squad deliverables completed on schedule

- Effective collaboration with other squads
